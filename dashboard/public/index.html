💬 Available Commands

Natural Language
  • ➕
    "Add buy groceries to my list" Creates a new todo item. You can include priority and category.
  • 📋
    "Show my todos" / "What's on my list?" Lists all active todo items with priorities.
  • ✅
    "Done with groceries" / "Mark groceries as complete" Completes a task and moves it to the Completed sheet.
  • 🗑️
    "Delete groceries from my list" Permanently removes a task from your list.
  • ✏️
    "Change groceries priority to high" Updates task properties like priority, category, or due date.
  • 📊
    "Give me a summary" / "How am I doing?" Shows overview with counts by priority and category.
  • 📄
    "Create a new sheet called Work Tasks" Creates a new tab/sheet in your spreadsheet.
